Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Quadcore ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our websites and products, including quadcore.app, DevStudio, and CELUX (celux.quadcore.app).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session duration, and browser/device type.
  • Account data — email address and profile information when you create an account.
  • Payment data — processed securely by our payment provider; we do not store card details.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — used to operate our services, remember preferences, and serve ads.

2. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our websites. These include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, used to understand how visitors interact with our sites.
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense, used to display relevant advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our sites or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our products (DevStudio and CELUX).
  • To process subscriptions and purchases.
  • To communicate with you about your account or support requests.
  • To display advertisements via Google AdSense on our free-tier services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

Quadcore does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Data shared with third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, Google AdSense) is governed by those services' own privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our products may integrate with or display content from third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. Relevant third parties include:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, object to or restrict processing, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@quadcore.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
